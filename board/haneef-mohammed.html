Executive Biography

Haneef Mohammed is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of H.E.M (Hustle Excellence Movement), a multi-division African technology and digital infrastructure group focused on building scalable platforms across media, AI, and software systems.

He is the architect behind the H.E.M ecosystem, a growing network of digital products and services designed to support Africa’s transformation in technology, communication, and intelligent systems. Under his leadership, H.E.M has evolved into a structured multi-brand organization including projects such as Cinexa Plus, Pinglo, and Ontrixa AI marketing systems.

Ecosystem Vision

"To build one of Africa’s most advanced technology ecosystems—uniting media, AI, communication, and enterprise tools into a single intelligent digital network under H.E.M Africa Group."

Haneef’s vision for H.E.M is centered on building a unified African tech ecosystem powered by cloud infrastructure, artificial intelligence, and modern software architecture. The group operates with a long-term focus on scalability, automation, and global competitiveness, with AWS-based infrastructure forming part of its technical foundation.

As a young technology founder, he represents a new generation of African builders focused on creating from the ground up rather than relying on imported systems. His work emphasizes innovation, rapid iteration, and ecosystem thinking—where each product connects into a larger digital network under the H.E.M umbrella.

Beyond product development, Haneef is also focused on building organizational structure, digital governance systems, and enterprise-grade platforms that allow H.E.M to grow into a fully integrated tech and media conglomerate.
