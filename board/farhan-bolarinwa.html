Executive Biography

Farhan Bolarinwa serves as the Chief of Communications for H.E.M. Africa Group. He is responsible for safeguarding the corporate reputation, managing external relations, and maintaining the authoritative voice of the Hustle Excellence Movement.

A key part of his portfolio involves coordinating outputs for the H.E.M. Media Network, ensuring that the company's journalism and press releases meet rigorous editorial standards. Farhan works closely with the executive team to align product launches, such as those for Pinglo and Cinexa+, with cohesive global messaging.

Whether managing stakeholder relations or developing crisis communication protocols, Farhan's leadership guarantees that H.E.M. Africa Group communicates with clarity, transparency, and impact.
