About H.E.M Africa

Hustle Excellence Movement

H.E.M Africa (Hustle Excellence Movement) is a technology and media company building the future of African digital innovation. We are focused on developing powerful platforms that connect people, deliver information, and unlock new opportunities across the continent.

Our Mission

To empower Africa through technology by creating scalable digital ecosystems in media, finance, entertainment, AI, and social platforms.

"We believe Africa is not the future — Africa is the present. H.E.M exists to accelerate that reality."

What We Build

H.E.M Africa is an ecosystem of interconnected platforms, including:

  • H.E.M News — A modern news platform delivering fast, reliable, and African-focused updates.
  • H.E.M Finance — A financial intelligence platform designed to improve financial awareness and access.
  • Cinexa+ — A digital entertainment and streaming experience built for African audiences.
  • Pinglo — A next-generation social platform focused on communication and community.

Each product is designed to work together, creating a unified digital experience.

Our Vision

To become one of Africa’s leading technology ecosystems, competing globally while solving local challenges through innovation.

We aim to build platforms that are:

  • Fast and scalable
  • Accessible across Africa
  • Powered by modern AI and cloud infrastructure
  • Designed for the next generation of users

Why H.E.M Exists

Africa is full of talent, creativity, and opportunity — but often lacks the digital infrastructure to support it at scale. H.E.M Africa exists to change that by building tools that help people:

  • Stay informed
  • Grow financially
  • Connect socially
  • Access entertainment
  • And create digital opportunities

Our Future

We are just getting started. H.E.M Africa is continuously expanding its ecosystem with new platforms, tools, and partnerships aimed at reshaping how Africans interact with technology.
