H.E.M Africa Privacy Commitment

We protect your information with transparency and purpose. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information from your use of our services, and technical data such as device identifiers, browser data, and usage analytics.

2. How We Use Information

  • Deliver and improve our platforms
  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Communicate service updates and support responses
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized activity

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure performance, and support essential platform functions.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with service providers, legal authorities when required, and business partners only as necessary to operate our services.

5. Data Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data. While we strive to protect user information, no system is absolutely secure.

Updated: May 31, 2026

Our policies are reviewed regularly to ensure compliance with emerging regulations and user expectations.

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at privacy@hemafrica.com.
