Privacy Policy

1. Regulatory Compliance

HEM Africa Group operates in strict accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and U.S. federal privacy standards. We ensure that data collected through HMN, Pinglo, Cinexa, and Ontrixa AI is processed with institutional integrity.

2. Data Sovereignty

We recognize the importance of data residency. Nigerian user data is managed under NDPC guidelines, while U.S. operations comply with relevant state-level consumer protections.
